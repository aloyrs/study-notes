# Math in Markdown

You can write inline math like this: $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$.

For block math, use double dollar signs:

$$
\int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \frac{1}{3}
$$

The first derivative of \( f(x) \) is:

$$
f'(x) = \frac{d}{dx} f(x)
$$

The second derivative of \( f(x) \) is:

$$
f''(x) = \frac{d^2}{dx^2} f(x)
$$
